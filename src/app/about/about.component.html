Our Story

VoceFemme all comes out of one idea: lifting one’s voice to inspire others to lift theirs. This is a collection of: up-in-coming women, funny women, running the show women, sing their hearts out women.

In honor of Women’s History Month, we are celebrating women who are trail-blazers in their field by creating an app to showcases these brilliant industry leaders. VoceFemme works as a database of CEOs to journalists from all over the world that can be searched by their Twitter handles or tags. Can’t find the funky fresh lady you’re looking for? We got you. Simply add them to the VoceFemme database and be part of the many women who continue to help VoceFemme grow and evolve, so we can be better. Every. Dang. Day.
