Our Story

VoceFemme all comes out of one idea: lifting one’s voice to inspire others to lift theirs. This is a collection of: up-and-coming women, funny women, running the show women, sing their hearts out women.

In honor of Women’s History Month, we celebrate women who are trailblazers in their field by creating an app to showcases these brilliant industry leaders. VoceFemme works as a Twitter database of CEOs to journalists from all over the world. Can’t find the funky fresh lady you’re looking for on our app? We got you. Simply add them to the VoceFemme database and be part of the many women who continue to help VoceFemme grow and evolve so that we can become better. Every. Dang. Day.
